Oral Presentation AAAI-26 | Singapore

"Tractable Sharpness-Aware Learning of Probabilistic Circuits"

Hrithik Suresh, Sahil Sidheekh, Vishnu Shreeram M. P., Sriraam Natarajan, Narayanan C. Krishnan

This work presents a hybrid training method for Probabilistic Circuits that combines the generalizability of EM with the speed of SGD. We implement a Hessian-based regularizer to guide models toward flatter optima, reducing overfitting. We prove that the Hessian trace (sharpness proxy) is efficiently computable for PCs, enabling closed-form updates.

Patent Filed Application No: 202541068350 | Filed: July 17, 2025

"Method, Apparatus, and System for Increasing Throughput in a Surface Mount Technology Assembly Line"

This invention presents a novel method for optimizing feeder arrangements in SMT pick-and-place machines. The algorithm increases components-per-hour (CPH) by at least 7% while meeting all manufacturing constraints. The approach combines Genetic Algorithms, Mixed-Integer Linear Programming (MILP), and Nonlinear Programming techniques.
