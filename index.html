About Me

I am a final-year B.Tech Data Science student at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Palakkad with a CGPA of 9.57/10. My research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning, Probabilistic Models, and Optimization.

I have research experience in Probabilistic Circuits, especially in Tractable Learning and Generalisation at the Laboratory of Statistical Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Our work on "Tractable Sharpness-Aware Learning of Probabilistic Circuits" has been accepted for Oral Presentation at AAAI-26 in Singapore.

I also have experience in Industrial Optimization, where I developed algorithms to optimize Bosch's SMT assembly lines, resulting in a patent filing (Application No: 202541068350).
